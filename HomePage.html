Extech CO24O

What's the idea: System to detect levels of oxygen/carbon dioxide and open windows when oxygen level drops or carbon dioxide level rises, thereby preventing accidental deaths of children or pets locked inside the car.

Product Description

  • Audible CO 2 warning alarm when concentration level exceeds high user set point.
  • Data Hold, Auto Power Off (with disable), Low battery indicator.
  • Can be programmed to roll down windows of cars incase of emergency.
  • Complete with six AAA batteries, USB cable, PC software, and soft case.
